Research

Research Areas & Vision

My research focuses on developing intelligent and data-driven systems that combine Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Internet of Things, TinyML and Wireless Sensor Networks to solve real-world environmental and smart city challenges.

Research Vision

My long-term research goal is to develop intelligent, energy-efficient and explainable AI systems capable of monitoring environmental conditions, assisting decision-making and improving quality of life through smart sensing technologies.

I am particularly interested in combining Artificial Intelligence with low-power embedded devices, Wireless Sensor Networks and Internet of Things technologies for sustainable smart environments.

Research Methodology

My research integrates data acquisition, sensor deployment, machine learning, deep learning, explainable AI, optimization techniques, and statistical analysis to build intelligent environmental monitoring systems.

I employ both simulation and real-world experimental validation, ensuring practical applicability and reproducibility of research outcomes.
