I am committed to creating an engaging learning environment where students develop strong analytical thinking, programming skills, and research aptitude through practical, project-based learning.

Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy emphasizes conceptual understanding, hands-on experimentation, critical thinking, and real-world applications. I encourage students to actively participate in problem solving, research, and collaborative learning.

I believe that integrating modern technologies, interactive coding sessions, and research-based assignments helps students build confidence and prepares them for both industry and academic careers.
