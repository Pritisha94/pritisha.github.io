About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering with research interests in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Internet of Things, TinyML, Wireless Sensor Networks, Environmental Monitoring, Smart Cities, Cyber Security, and Reinforcement Learning.

My research primarily focuses on intelligent sensing systems, air quality monitoring, explainable AI, deep learning, data analytics, and sustainable smart environments.

I enjoy teaching undergraduate and postgraduate students, supervising research, developing innovative AI solutions, and collaborating on interdisciplinary research projects.
